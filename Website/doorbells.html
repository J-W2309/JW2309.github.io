Doorbells


Smart Doorbells are an alternative to your standard traditional doorbell. They have features such as camera's, microphone's, and the ability to check your door no matter your location. Everyone has missed a delivery or a visitor when out however with a Smart Doorbell you can see who is at your door through an app. This is highly convenient and these devices are becoming extremely popular due to this. However there are some privacy and security risks in owning one.


Phyiscal Security Risks


Unlike most other smart devices the Smart Doorbell is located on the outside of your home. This unfortunately makes them a target for theft. Thankfully the majority of these devices include camera microphones and tracking information which is all relayed to your mobile app meaning any incident that occours can be recorded and followed. this is a huge deterance for thieves however it doesnt mean your completely safe. There isnt much you can do other than install your smart doorbell in a secure way and make sure you have enabled all of the security mentioned above.
