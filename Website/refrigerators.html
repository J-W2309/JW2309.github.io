Refrigerators


Smart Refrigerators have become quite a sought after applicance in recent years. They arent all that different from your standard refrigerator other than the fact you can control your home and connect to other devices through it while keeping the basic functions the same. Smart Refrigerators may seem like one of the safer smart devices in your home, its in one place, cant be stolen etc. However, there are still some risks involved in owning one.


Phyiscal Security Risks


Physical

There isnt too much to talk about when it comes to the physical security of a Smart Refrigerator. Compared to the majority of smart devices this one is usually a large object and installed within a household. There is slim to none chances of theft in that aspect. however one issue could occour that an intruder may be able to gain access to the rest of your Smart home using this device. due to this even a Smart Refrigerator needs a password to protect it.

Technical Security Risks


Wi-Fi

When it comes to Smart Devices Wi-Fi is one of the most important aspects. it is also one that needs to have a close eye kept on.

Hacking

Hacking is still a persistent threat no matter what smart devie your are using. Even a smart Refrigerator can be hacked. the same precautions can be taken to stop this such as keeping the device up to date with the latest security as well as keeping your Wi-Fi secure. This guide will not go over every attack that could occour however there are many external tests that have been conducted and published on these devices.
